FAQ

Questions, answered plainly

Is VocabLearn free?

Yes — completely free, supported by ads. There is no paid version. You start with two learning languages included; if you want to add another language, or jump ahead to a higher CEFR level before finishing your current one, you can unlock it by watching a short rewarded ad. Watching is always your choice.

Does it work offline?

Fully. All 3,600 words and their translations are bundled inside the app — nothing is fetched from a server. Your progress is stored on your phone, and pronunciation is generated on-device. Every part of learning works without an internet connection.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no email, no password and no cloud sync. You open the app and start learning.

Why do I see ads?

Ads are what keep the app free. They're served by Google AdMob and kept out of your way: a full-screen ad appears only when you leave a quiz results screen — never during a quiz. Rewarded ads are entirely optional and only play when you choose to watch one.

Which languages are supported?

Thirteen, both as the interface language and as a language you can learn: English, Turkish, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Hungarian, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Chinese and Russian. You pick a native language and learn any of the others.

Is my data private?

Your learning data — the words you add, your progress and settings — stays on your phone, and you never create an account. The app uses Google services (AdMob for ads, and Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for usage insights and crash reports), which process some device data on Google's side. We don't sell your data. The full details are in our privacy policy.
